Plant Automation


LNI Productions is a leader in delivering advanced food processing and bakery automation solutions that seamlessly integrate cutting-edge technology into modern production environments. We provide end-to-end automation services, from targeted process improvements to fully automated production lines, designed to enhance efficiency and scalability.

By leveraging robotics, IoT-enabled monitoring, and intelligent control systems, we help food manufacturers achieve consistent product quality, higher yields, and optimized operational performance.

Our solutions significantly reduce manual dependency while improving food safety and process reliability, enabling businesses to stay competitive in an increasingly demanding and fast-paced food industry.

Operation Planning


Efficient operation planning is essential for meeting production goals while maintaining quality and minimizing costs. At Autobake Productions, our operation planning experts collaborate closely with your team to design actionable strategies.


From raw material sourcing to finished product delivery, we ensure that each step of your bakery's operation is optimized. Our holistic approach encompasses demand forecasting and inventory management while streamlining logistics so that you navigate challenges confidently.

Training & Development


The success of any bakery relies on a skilled workforce. Autobake Productions offers comprehensive training and development programs tailored to the bakery industry. Our experienced trainers cover every baking-related topic, from operational best practices to safety and hygiene protocols.


These hands-on workshops and customized training modules empower staff to operate equipment efficiently, maintain high-quality standards, and swiftly adapt to evolving technology and trends.
